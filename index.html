    About Us




    EuphoriaHsüan Studio is an Internet comprehensive studio founded by Wu Chenghsüan in July 2023. EuphoriaHsüan Studio was formerly known as H-Estrella Team. H-Estrella Team was an independent video game music studio established by Wu Chenghsüan in May 2022. The studio was dissolved in July 2023, but was reorganized as EuphoriaHsüan Studio shortly thereafter .

    Euphoria Hsüan Studio was named after Wu Chenghsüan, Hsüan is Wade–Giles of the Chinese character "軒", and Euphoria represents happiness. The original intention of EuphoriaHsüan Studio was to develop open source projects, to bring users a comfortable and perfect experience, and to provide more technical and financial support for open source projects through continuous self-improvement.

    Representative Projects of this studio are "Dirty Electro", "Project-KurokoWiki", "EuphoriaHsüan Studio Workshop" and "Project-Hsüan".







    Projects




      

    •  Dirty Electro


    Dirty Electro is a project founded by H-Estrella Team on June 6, 2022 in Amsterdam, the Netherlands . The Project focuses on music communication, supplemented by online forums and Web tools.

    The website opened the Alpha test in Amsterdam, the Netherlands on June 6, 2022, and the Beta closed test on the 24th.

    In January 2023, an open test for the public was launched in Nanchang, Jiangxi Province, China .

    In April 2023, the Final test will start in Taipei, Taiwan .

    On May 1, 2023, Project Dirty Electro is temporarily closed.

    On July 24, 2023, the original development and operation team H-ESTRELLA TEAM was disbanded, and Project Dirty Electro was taken over by the new development and operation team Hsuan (EuphoriaHsuan Studio or EH Studio).

    On July 24, 2023, the Dirty Electro main site redeveloped a new version and named it EH Version.

    On July 27, 2023, the Dirty Electro substation T Social(T-Social) was first developed, and version 0.1 was released the next day. On the 30th, the official version of T-Social changed its name to Electronic Post(Electronic Post).

    On August 4, 2023, the new version of the Dirty Electro main site, EH Version 1.0.0, was released.



    Website




      

    •  Project-Kuroko Wiki


    Kuroko Wiki is an encyclopedia website similar to Wikipedia. This website was originally developed by H-Estrella Team on July 25, 2022.

    Kuroko Wiki takes its name from the anime character Shirai Kuroko, a character in the A Certain Magical Index and A Certain Scientific Railgun franchises. Kuroko Wiki was originally created to introduce the anime character Shirai Kuroko, but H-Estrella Team discovered its potential and developed it as an encyclopedia website.

    On August 12 of the same year, the first version of Kuroko Wiki ver.Alpha was developed and internal testing started.

    On August 29, 2022, the website will release version 1.0.0. On the same day, H-Estrella Team suspended development of the Kuroko Wiki website and stopped support for Project-Kuroko Wiki.

    On July 24, 2023, H-Estrella Team was disbanded, and all its development projects, including Project-Kuroko Wiki, were handed over to the reorganized EuphoriaHsuan Studio.

    •  Project-Hsüan


    Project-Hsüan (also known as Hsuan™Project-Beta) is a website developed by H-Estrella Team starting on January 30, 2023. The main function is a personal profile. But after only 5 days, the website stopped updating on February 5, 2023.

    On May 1, 2023, H-Estrella Team completely stopped the development support for Project-Hsüan.
